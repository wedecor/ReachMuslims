## Full Description

**Reach Muslim is a lead management app designed for Muslim matrimony services. Manage leads, track follow-ups, and communicate efficiently via Call and WhatsApp.**

### Key Features:
- **Lead Management**: Create, edit, and organize leads efficiently
- **Follow-up Tracking**: Never miss a follow-up with scheduled reminders
- **Communication**: Quick access to Call and WhatsApp for direct contact
- **Dashboard Analytics**: Track conversion rates and pipeline health
- **Role-Based Access**: Admin and Sales user roles with appropriate permissions
- **Regional Management**: Organize leads by region (India/USA)

### Perfect For:
- Matrimony service providers
- Sales teams managing matrimony leads
- Administrators tracking conversion metrics
